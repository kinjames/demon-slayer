Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Tanjiro Kamado is a kind-hearted and intelligent boy who lives with his family in the mountains.
He became his family's breadwinner after his father's death, making trips to the nearby village to sell charcoal.
Everything changed when he came home one day to discover that his family was attacked and slaughtered by a demon.
Tanjiro Kamado, who strived to become a demon slayer after his family was slaughtered and his younger sister Nezuko turned into a demon.
Tanjiro became a demon slayer to protect his litte sister Nezuko and also seek vengeance for his family.
